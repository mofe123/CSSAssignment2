About KOKORO

Kokoro is a rod ‐ like shaped crunchy Nigerian snack, popular in some parts of Nigeria.
It is made with corn meal/corn flour and usually sold by street hawkers.
I think it is very popular in Abeokuta compare to other states cause i rarely see it in other states.

  3. On an adventure with friends.
